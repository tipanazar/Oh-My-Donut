Coffee Lab at Donuts is a combo of knowledge, skill and coffee. The tools of the "coffee laboratory" are a theoretical part with visual slides, cupping (tasting) of several monosorts: Ethiopia, Kenya, etc. (and one mix) and a master class on coffee preparation methods and late-art, where everyone can try to create distinguishable drawings on a cappuccino froth. If you love coffee, then come to one of the coffee houses for a cup, if you love coffee with all your heart - visit the Coffee Laboratory!
